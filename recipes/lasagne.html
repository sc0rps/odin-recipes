Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a classic Italian baked pasta dish, consisting of layered flat, wide pasta sheets, typically alternating with fillings and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Beef mince

Steps

  1. Cook beef mince
  2. Start first layer of lasagne sheet and add mince on top
  3. Repeat with numerous layers
  4. On the final layer add cheese on top and heat
