Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta, typically made from durum wheat semolina and water

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Special Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti pasta
  2. Throughly mix with the special sauce prepared beforehand
  3. Add salt for the finishing touches
